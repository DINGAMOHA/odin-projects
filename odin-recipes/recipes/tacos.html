tacos recipe

Description

Tacos are a vibrant, flavorful dish that brings together a mix of textures and bold tastes in one handheld bite. At their core, tacos consist of a soft or crispy tortilla—usually made from corn or wheat—filled with a variety of ingredients. The filling can be anything from seasoned meat like beef, chicken to beans, grilled vegetables or even seafood.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  3. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  4. Cover slow cooker. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken with two forks.
